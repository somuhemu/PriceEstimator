"What does building a quality digital application cost?” - by far the most
asked question, if we must believe our Account Managers.

We put it to the test and asked a random group of people that particular
question. Let’s say we got the most varied answers; from “I don’t know,
like the cost of a bag of chips” to “well that would probably cost me a
brand new Ferrari, or two”.

So, to help all misunderstandings out of this world and to relieve our
sales guys and girls, we decided to create a digital application cost
calculator.
